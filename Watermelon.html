Watermelon Gazpacho

Ingredients:

  1. 4 cups cubed seeded watermelon
  2. 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, seeded and chopped
  3. 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  4. 1/2 English (seedless) cucumber - peeled, seeded and cubed
  5. 2 tablespoons minced shallot
  6. 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  7. 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  8. 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, or to taste
  9. 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  10. 2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese
  11. 2 tablespoons minced fresh cilantro

Directions:

  1. Combine watermelon, tomatoes, red pepper, cucumber, shallot, lime juice, vinegar, salt and pepper in a high-powered blender.
    Blend until smooth, 20 to 30 seconds.
    Refrigerate for at least 1 hour to allow flavors to meld and soup to chill.
  2. Stir gazpacho before serving, as it has a tendency to separate, and divide between 4 bowls. Top with feta cheese and cillantro
